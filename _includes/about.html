About Us

Killer Rabbit Labs?

Yes! We're a Python shop that embraces the fun and quirky culture of the Python community.

Seeing as Python got it's name from Monty Python's Flying Circus we decided to embrace an iconic scene from Monty Python and the Holy Grail, the killer rabbit. Like this rabbit we in KRL think we can tackle any project!

Who are we?

We are developers with a passion for code and product. We focus on Open Source technologies and industry standard methodologies. Together Froilán Irizarry and Sasha Mendez have more than 15 years experience developing and managing software projects, in a variety of industries including telecomm, insurance, and government.
